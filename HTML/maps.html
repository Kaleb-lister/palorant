Master the Battlefield – Explore Palorant's Maps!

Each battlefield has its own unique challenges. Get to know the map well, master the strategy, and dominate the fight!


Nexus Prime


A futuristic metropolis suspended above the clouds, Nexus Prime is a battleground filled with towering skyscrapers, neon-lit pathways, and dynamic ziplines connecting different sectors. The central area, known as The Core, features an open space where players can engage in intense mid-range combat, while the underground tunnels provide sneaky rotation options for flanking enemies. Advanced holographic cover systems shift throughout the match, making adaptability a key skill for victory.


Ashen Wastes


Once a thriving industrial hub, Ashen Wastes is now a desolate, volcanic landscape covered in molten rivers and crumbling structures. Players must navigate through smoke-filled corridors, elevated pipelines, and unstable bridges that can collapse under heavy fire. With limited visibility due to constant ash storms, communication and teamwork are essential to surviving the deadly choke points and securing objectives.


Titan's Ruin


A fallen ancient city once ruled by titanic beings, Titan’s Ruin is an overgrown battleground filled with colossal statues, hidden catacombs, and vertical cliffs perfect for sniping. The map is divided into three key areas: The Forgotten Plaza, an open battlefield littered with remnants of an ancient war; The Abyss, a deep ravine with risky jump routes; and The Sanctum, an enclosed temple filled with tight corridors perfect for close-quarters combat.


Mirage Bay


A tropical paradise turned warzone, Mirage Bay is an island map with hidden underwater tunnels, cliffside sniper nests, and abandoned resorts that serve as key control points. The map’s central Sunken Plaza constantly changes due to the rising and falling tides, making every round feel different. Players must use zipline bridges, secret caves, and dense palm forests to outmaneuver opponents and control the battlefield.


Cryo Facility-09


A high-security research base buried deep in the Arctic, Cryo Facility-09 is a frozen battlefield where temperature fluctuations cause ice walls to shatter, revealing new pathways mid-game. The facility is divided into the Main Lab, a spacious area filled with cryogenic chambers; the Reactor Room, where unstable energy fields can disrupt vision; and the Hangar, an open-air escape route with minimal cover but high mobility potential. Players must balance speed, strategy, and environmental awareness to dominate in this dynamic, frozen wasteland.
